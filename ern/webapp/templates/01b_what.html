What do we ask of you during the research?

  1. You are asked to do five tasks as part of a game.
  2. You have to come up with a story between 5 and 20 sentences.
  3. You are asked to change the order of the sentences of the story
  4. You estimate the difficulty of reconstructing the original story
  5. You are asked to rate the model's reconstruction of your reordering
  6. You are asked to rate the model's reconstruction of a random reordering
